Tourist Attractions in Oman

Discover the breathtaking landmarks, vibrant cities, and cultural experiences Oman has to offer.

Famous Landmarks

Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque

One of the largest mosques in the world, known for its stunning architecture and intricate interiors.

Wahiba Sands

An iconic desert destination, offering dune bashing, camel rides, and starry night camping experiences.

Mutrah Souk

A bustling marketplace in Muscat filled with spices, jewelry, textiles, and authentic Omani souvenirs.

5-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Muscat

Visit the Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque, stroll along Mutrah Corniche, and shop at Mutrah Souk. Dine at Kargeen Caf√© for a taste of authentic Omani cuisine.

Day 2: Nizwa

Explore Nizwa Fort and the traditional Nizwa Souk. Stay at the Golden Tulip Nizwa Hotel and savor local delights at Al-Masharef Restaurant.

Day 3: Wahiba Sands

Experience dune bashing and camel trekking. Spend the night at a desert camp and enjoy a traditional Bedouin dinner under the stars.

Day 4: Jebel Akhdar

Hike through the "Green Mountain," visit ancient villages, and enjoy breathtaking views. Stay at the Anantara Al Jabal Al Akhdar Resort.

Day 5: Sur & Ras Al Jinz

Explore the coastal city of Sur, see dhow shipyards, and visit Ras Al Jinz Turtle Reserve for a unique turtle-watching experience.
